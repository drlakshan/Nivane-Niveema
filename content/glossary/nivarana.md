# Nīvaraṇa

Pāli term for the hindrances: mental obstacles that block collectedness and insight.

## Standard five
- sensual desire
- ill will
- sloth and torpor
- restlessness and worry
- doubt

## Main references in this corpus
- Sermon 31
- Sermon 15
