# Consciousness (Viññāṇa)

## Concept
Consciousness is treated in this corpus as dependently arisen rather than as a permanent essence. Major discussions connect consciousness with name-and-form, the six sense-based consciousnesses, and the cessation dynamic relevant to Nibbāna.

## Key lines of discussion
- Consciousness and name-and-form mutually condition one another.
- There are six consciousnesses tied to the six sense spheres.
- Consciousness is not a substantial self.
- Some sermons discuss *anidassana viññāṇa* (non-manifestative consciousness).
