# Name-and-Form

## Concept
Name-and-form is one of the most important structural themes in the corpus. It is closely linked with consciousness and helps explain how lived experience and saṃsāric continuity are configured.

## Key lines of discussion
- reciprocal relation between consciousness and name-and-form
- cessation of one linked to cessation of the other
- practical significance for understanding the experiential world
