# Dependent Origination

## Concept
A central theme of the corpus. Dependent origination is treated not as abstract metaphysics alone, but as a practical key to understanding arising, cessation, bondage, and release.

## Key lines of discussion
- conditional arising and conditional cessation
- the link structure beginning with ignorance and formations
- consciousness and name-and-form as a crucial reciprocal relation
- insight into dependent origination as a path to disentanglement
