# Saṅkhāra

## Concept
Saṅkhāra is a major interpretive term in the corpus. Depending on context it bears the sense of preparations, formations, activities, or fabrications relevant to dependent arising and the stilling that culminates in Nibbāna.

## Key lines of discussion
- saṅkhāra in dependent origination
- the stilling of all preparations
- relation to intentional activity and constructing tendencies
