# Cessation

## Concept
Cessation is a core key to understanding Nibbāna in this corpus. The sermons often explain liberation through cessation of conditions rather than through positing an eternal essence.

## Key lines of discussion
- cessation of craving
- cessation within dependent origination
- cessation of name-and-form and consciousness in reciprocal dependence
- cessation as stilling and peace
