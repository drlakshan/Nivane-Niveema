# Five Aggregates

## Concept
The five aggregates provide a framework for analysing what is taken as a self. In this corpus they are often treated in relation to emptiness, instability, and deconstruction of identification.

## The five
1. Form
2. Feeling
3. Perception
4. Preparations / formations
5. Consciousness
