# Satipaṭṭhāna

## Concept
The sermons use the *Satipaṭṭhāna* framework as a practical doorway to insight. In later sermons this becomes especially important for themes like hindrances, mental observation, and right effort.

## Key lines of discussion
- mindfulness as clear knowing of arising and passing
- *dhammānupassanā* as an analytical contemplative framework
- hindrances as objects of understanding and abandonment
