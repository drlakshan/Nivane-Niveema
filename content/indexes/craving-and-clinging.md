# Craving and Clinging

## Concept
Craving and clinging are central to bondage and continuity in saṃsāra. The sermons repeatedly connect Nibbāna with the destruction of craving and with disentanglement from grasping.

## Key lines of discussion
- craving as fuel for becoming
- clinging as appropriation and holding on
- release through dispassion and relinquishment
