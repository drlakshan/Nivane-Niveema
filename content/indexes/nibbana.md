# Nibbāna

## Concept
The whole sermon series is oriented around clarifying the meaning of Nibbāna from the early discourses. It is approached as cessation, relinquishment, dispassion, and the stilling of all preparations.

## Key lines of discussion
- destruction of craving
- cessation and dispassion
- stilling of all preparations
- relinquishment of acquisitions and supports
