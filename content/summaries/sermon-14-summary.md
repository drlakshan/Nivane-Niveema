# Sermon 14 Summary

## Main themes
- the *Dhātuvibhaṅgasutta*
- consciousness as tending to dwell or abide
- non-manifestative consciousness

## Why this matters
Useful for questions about the nature of consciousness, its support, and its transformation in relation to detachment.
