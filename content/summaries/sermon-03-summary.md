# Sermon 3 Summary

## Main themes
- dependent arising
- the vortex between consciousness and name-and-form
- cessation through breaking that reciprocal support

## Why this matters
This is one of the best starting points in the corpus for questions about consciousness, name-and-form, and the structure of saṃsāric continuity.
