# Sermon 31 Summary

## Main themes
- *satipaṭṭhāna*
- *dhammānupassanā*
- hindrances as objects of mindful comprehension
- relation between hindrances and right effort

## Why this matters
This is the main entry point in the corpus for practical discussion of the hindrances and their abandoning.
